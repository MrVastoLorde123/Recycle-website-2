What Can You Recycle?

Plastics

Check your local guidelines for what plastics can be recycled. Common recyclable plastics include water bottles and containers.

Paper

Most paper products are recyclable, including newspapers, magazines, and cardboard. Be sure to remove contaminants like food residue.

Metals

Aluminum cans, tin cans, and metal lids can usually be recycled. Check with local facilities for more detailed instructions.
